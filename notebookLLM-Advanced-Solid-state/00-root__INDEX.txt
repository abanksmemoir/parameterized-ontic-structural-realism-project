=== KEY CONCEPTS ===

- Parameterized Ontic Structural Realism (POSR): "Real" is an honorific for
  structural regions of the lattice of well-formed hypotheticals that do
  nontrivial empirical work. Platonism as navigational medium.

- Everything is a parameter: Every choice in the graph — from logic (Layer 0)
  to the CKM phase (Layer 9) — is a parameter subtyped as axiom, structural,
  kinematic, dynamical, boundary, or convention.

- Dependency types: logical (cannot be formulated otherwise), conventional
  (standard but alternatives exist), contingent (depends on conditions not
  internally guaranteed).

- The iε prescription (P057): The most consequential hidden parameter.
  Determines the causal arrow of the entire Standard Model. Physically
  justified only in U(1) electromagnetism via the Wheeler-Feynman absorber
  condition.

- The time-symmetry debt: The retarded propagator is chosen by hand in every
  gauge sector but structurally justified only in one. The five-agent research
  investigation mapped the shape of this debt across all sectors.

- The experimental origin: Wheeler-Feynman absorber theory predicts that under
  specific absorber-incomplete conditions (small antenna, atmospheric
  absorption), advanced electromagnetic waves should be detectable. NMK 2015
  (Phys Rev D) made this quantitative. Bajlo's contested 2016-2017
  replication observed signals matching NMK's predictions. The dependency
  graph is the theoretical map for this experimental program.
