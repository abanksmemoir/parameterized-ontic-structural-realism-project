# Parameterized Ontic Structural Realism

**Standard Model Structural Dependency Graph**

The Standard Model of particle physics decomposed into a dependency graph where each node declares its presuppositions, its outputs, and its parametric degrees of freedom. The graph runs from the choice of logical axioms (Layer 0) to the specific particle content and coupling constants (Layer 9). Every layer is a functor: axioms in, structural consequences out.

## Structure

- `sm_dependency_graph.json` — Machine-readable dependency graph (10 layers, 30 nodes)
- `sm_dependency_graph.md` — Human-readable specification with full annotations

## Philosophical Commitment

"Real" is an honorific bestowed on regions of the lattice of well-formed hypotheticals that do nontrivial empirical work. The Platonism is instrumental — it provides navigational medium without ontological commitment to any particular corner.
